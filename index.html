our services

Medical Reports

AYUSH clinics provide detailed medical reports that include diagnoses, treatment plans, and progress updates. These reports highlight natural remedies, lifestyle recommendations, and holistic patient evaluations.

Scans and Prescriptions

AYUSH clinics use traditional diagnostic methods like pulse diagnosis and Nadi Pariksha. They provide personalized prescriptions, including herbal remedies, dietary plans, and lifestyle changes for holistic health.

Online Yoga Sessions

AYUSH clinics offer online yoga sessions focusing on physical and mental wellness. These guided practices promote relaxation, flexibility, and overall health from the comfort of home.

Vehicle Arrangement

AYUSH clinics offer vehicle services for patients during critical situations, ensuring fast transport from nearby areas. This ensures timely access to necessary treatments in emergencies.

Clinic selection

AYUSH platforms provide an easy-to-use feature for selecting nearby clinics based on location. This helps patients quickly find the closest clinic for timely and convenient healthcare services.

Natural Remedies

Natural remedies use plant-based treatments and traditional methods from Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy. These remedies focus on promoting healing, boosting immunity, and improving overall health.
